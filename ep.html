EP Analytics

Overview

Challenge

Design a responsive website for San Diego business EP Analytics who provide expertise and software for high-performance computing.

Background

At MiraCosta college I was selected by my professors for a student internship with EP Analytics and California Community Colleges. The internship initially focused on cyber-security and later shifted to a UI redesign of the company's website. During this latter portion of the internship, I interviewed company leadership , presented a design-direction rationale, created high-fidelity mockups in Adobe XD, researched and tested multiple content management systems, advocated for a transition of the company site from WordPress to Webflow, and began development in Webflow. Post-internship EP Analytics hired me on a contract to finish the design and development of the company site.

Tools

Adobe XD, Photoshop, and Webflow.

Role

UI Designer and Front-end Developer
